A crowdsource learning community is an online platform where people come together to share their knowledge and learn from each other. This can be done through a variety of activities, such as:
- Asking and answering questions: Members can ask questions about any topic they are interested in, and other members can provide answers and insights.
- Sharing resources: Members can share articles, videos, podcasts, and other resources that they find helpful.
- Participating in discussions: Members can discuss topics of interest with other members, and learn from each other's perspectives.

- Access to wealth of knowledge.
- Personalised learning.
- Flexible learning.
- Networking opportunities.

Anyone can join a crowdsource learning community, regardless of their age, background, or education level. Communities are open to people from all walks of life.
